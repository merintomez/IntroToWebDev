Restaurant Description

"We're known for our rich and flavourful meats, which are smoked for up to 12 hours in our wood-burning oven to ensure a delicious and unforgettable taste. From smoked sandwiches, wraps, and burgers to tender and juicy smoked ribs, chicken dishes, and succulent seafood, you'll be sure to find something on our menu that will satisfy your barbeque-related cravings. We smoke all sorts of ingredients and menu items, from pork and beef to tomato sauce and even apples. Of course, we offer a wide array of meat-free dishes including various salads, veggie wraps, poutine, and our daily soup special. Our food is always made from scratch and never frozen."
